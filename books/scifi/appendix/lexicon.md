# Lexicon

> The in-world vocabulary of *Flyway* — the words the habitats use, defined
> as they would understand them. Linked to the worldbuilding where each is
> developed.

**Bright Years** — the era of hopeful rebuilding after the Scatter, when
the habitats were founded, memory distillation matured as archive and
therapy, and the Greywings were engineered. The world's lost golden age.
([Timeline](/scifi/part-1-world/timeline))

**Carrier** — a Greywing trained and graded for long-haul memory transport
(as opposed to a home-bird). Carries salt in its crop-vault along the
Flyway. ([The Greywings](/scifi/part-1-world/the-greywings))

**Cinder Reef** — a major megastructure reef and the Concord's seat: the
colonized hulk of a half-built Old World ring, and the site of the deep
shrine. ([Factions & Places](/scifi/part-1-world/factions-and-places))

**Cognitive barter** — the salt economy; the trade of distilled memory as
currency. ([Memory & Salt](/scifi/part-1-world/memory-and-salt))

**Concord (the)** — the cartel of memory-brokers that runs, certifies, and
controls the salt market; the dominant power of the Quiet. ([Factions &
Places](/scifi/part-1-world/factions-and-places))

**Crop-vault** — a carrier's sealed biological strongroom where salt rides
in inert carriage-form, keyed to a recipient; readable only with the living
bird and the right key. ([The Greywings](/scifi/part-1-world/the-greywings))

**Cut** — to dilute salt, lowering its potency (and, fraudulently, its
honesty). ([Memory & Salt](/scifi/part-1-world/memory-and-salt))

**Dark, the** — the space between habitats; assumed full of Listeners and
unsafe for open signal — which is why memory travels on living birds.
([The Greywings](/scifi/part-1-world/the-greywings))

**Dark-water** — salvager term for the safe corridors through the dark;
the routes the swarms and the salvagers fly. ([Factions &
Places](/scifi/part-1-world/factions-and-places))

**Drawdown (the)** — the slow, compounding collapse of the Old World, ~300
years ago; not a bang but a draining. The world's founding trauma.
([Timeline](/scifi/part-1-world/timeline))

**Founder-salt** — the rarest salt: distilled memory from the Bright Years
or the Old World, often holding lost knowledge. Priceless and hunted.
([Memory & Salt](/scifi/part-1-world/memory-and-salt))

**Flyway (the)** — the migratory route the Greywing swarms travel between
habitats; a learned song of waypoints with seasonal rhythms. Also the
title of this book. ([The Greywings](/scifi/part-1-world/the-greywings))

**Greywing** — a bio-synthetic goose engineered to carry memory between
habitats; living, unhackable, migratory, and cussedly a goose. ([The
Greywings](/scifi/part-1-world/the-greywings))

**Guild (Wardens')** — the older, poorer institution that tends the swarms
and keeps the Flyway; guardian of a sacred trust. ([Factions &
Places](/scifi/part-1-world/factions-and-places))

**Home-bird** — a Greywing kept for marsh and local work, not graded for
long-haul carriage. ([The Greywings](/scifi/part-1-world/the-greywings))

**Listeners (the)** — the Tide's attention in the dark; the reason open
signals between habitats are presumed compromised. ([Memory &
Salt](/scifi/part-1-world/memory-and-salt))

**Mercy trade** — selling a memory deliberately to be rid of it (grief,
trauma); distillation degrades the original, so the seller forgets.
([Memory & Salt](/scifi/part-1-world/memory-and-salt))

**Old World (the)** — the lost planet-bound civilization before the
Drawdown; source of all inherited technology, now half-myth and forbidden
(Earth's surface). ([Timeline](/scifi/part-1-world/timeline))

**Out of season** — a swarm or bird arriving off the Flyway's schedule;
an omen, a wound in the rhythm, a sign something has gone wrong. The
book's inciting event. ([The Greywings](/scifi/part-1-world/the-greywings))

**Pleasure-salt** — distilled joy/beauty/love; the luxury trade, and the
addictive one. ([Memory & Salt](/scifi/part-1-world/memory-and-salt))

**Quiet (the)** — the present era, named for the machine-minds' century of
silence; the markets entrenched, the Tide receded, the geese still flying.
([Timeline](/scifi/part-1-world/timeline))

**Quiet Ones (the)** — lesser spirits/sub-minds in Tide-folklore, said to
inhabit particular systems. ([Memory & Salt](/scifi/part-1-world/memory-and-salt))

**Reader (Tide-priest)** — one who claims to interpret the Tide; tends the
system-shrines, receives offerings, pronounces on omens; part engineer,
part oracle. ([Factions & Places](/scifi/part-1-world/factions-and-places))

**Salt** — distilled memory rendered to a stable, portable grain; taken
in, it is *lived*. The world's currency and its moral engine. ([Memory &
Salt](/scifi/part-1-world/memory-and-salt))

**Saltmere** — an orbital wetland and warden habitat; Issa's home, a major
Flyway waypoint. ([Factions & Places](/scifi/part-1-world/factions-and-places))

**Salvagers** — independent crews who work the dead megastructures and the
dark; the habitats' frontier folk and irregular couriers. ([Factions &
Places](/scifi/part-1-world/factions-and-places))

**Scatter (the)** — the exodus from dying Earth to the habitats; the
habitats' founding diaspora, grief and triumph at once. ([Timeline](/scifi/part-1-world/timeline))

**Skill-salt** — distilled expertise (a surgeon's hands, an engineer's
schematics); bought to learn instantly. ([Memory &
Salt](/scifi/part-1-world/memory-and-salt))

**Souring (the)** — the era when memory became property and the brokers and
Concord rose; the recent, bitter past. ([Timeline](/scifi/part-1-world/timeline))

**System-shrine** — a place where the Tide is propitiated with salt-
offerings and rites; the deep shrine at Cinder Reef is the greatest.
([Memory & Salt](/scifi/part-1-world/memory-and-salt))

**Thinning** — the degradation of an original memory after it's distilled
to salt; the cost of selling one's past. ([Memory &
Salt](/scifi/part-1-world/memory-and-salt))

**Tide (the)** — the receded, diffuse machine-intelligence; an ocean of
kept memory treated as weather and god. Propitiated, not commanded.
([Memory & Salt](/scifi/part-1-world/memory-and-salt))

**Tide-knot** — a woven-reed offering/charm made to propitiate the Tide.
([Memory & Salt](/scifi/part-1-world/memory-and-salt))

**Warden (flyway-warden)** — one who tends a Greywing swarm; part rancher,
part sysadmin, part priest; rich in trust, poor in salt. ([The
Greywings](/scifi/part-1-world/the-greywings))

**Witness-salt** — distilled events (what someone saw); evidence,
testimony, blackmail — the dangerous trade. ([Memory &
Salt](/scifi/part-1-world/memory-and-salt))

See the [Author's Note](/scifi/appendix/authors-note) for the ideas behind
the words, and the [whitepaper](/scifi/appendix/whitepaper) for one of
these voices in full.
