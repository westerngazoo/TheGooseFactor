# Unit I: Atomic Structure

Everything in chemistry begins with the structure of the atom and, in particular, the geometry of where its electrons can be. The quantum numbers are an index into a catalogue of **orbital shapes** — spherical $s$ orbitals, dumbbell $p$ orbitals, cloverleaf $d$ orbitals — and those shapes will dictate how atoms bond in Unit III. We treat orbitals as the angular, oriented objects they are.
