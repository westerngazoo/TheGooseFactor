# Polynomial and Rational Functions

This section introduces polynomial and rational functions.

## Polynomial Functions

A polynomial function of degree $n$ is of the form $P(x) = a_n x^n + a_{n-1} x^{n-1} + \dots + a_1 x + a_0$.

## Rational Functions

A rational function is the ratio of two polynomials: $R(x) = \frac{P(x)}{Q(x)}$ where $Q(x) \neq 0$.
