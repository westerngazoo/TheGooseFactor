# Exponential and Logarithmic Functions

This section covers exponential and logarithmic functions.

## Exponential Functions

An exponential function has the form $f(x) = a^x$ where $a > 0$ and $a \neq 1$.

## Logarithmic Functions

The inverse of an exponential function is a logarithmic function: $y = \log_a(x) \iff a^y = x$.
