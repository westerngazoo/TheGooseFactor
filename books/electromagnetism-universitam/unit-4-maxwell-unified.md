# Unit IV: Maxwell Unified in GA

This is the keystone unit. We assemble the electromagnetic field as a single object and show that the four Maxwell equations are the grade-by-grade components of one equation in **spacetime algebra (STA)**:

$$ \nabla F = J. $$

Everything in Units I–III was preparation for reading this sentence and unpacking it back into the familiar four laws.

## Planned Sections

- Spacetime algebra: the basis vectors $\gamma_\mu$ and the spacetime metric.
- Assembling the field bivector $F$ from $\mathbf{E}$ and $\mathbf{B}$.
- The spacetime vector derivative $\nabla$.
- Deriving $\nabla F = J$ and projecting it back into the four Maxwell equations.
- Charge conservation $\nabla\cdot J = 0$ as an immediate corollary.
- Worked examples and persona callouts matching the Calculus Universitam Unit I quality bar.
