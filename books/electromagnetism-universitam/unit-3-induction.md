# Unit III: Electromagnetic Induction

Induction is where electricity and magnetism stop being separate subjects. A changing magnetic field drives an electric field, and vice versa. In Geometric Algebra this coupling is the statement that the time derivative mixes the vector and bivector parts of the single field $F$ — the first concrete sign that $\mathbf{E}$ and $\mathbf{B}$ are facets of one object.
