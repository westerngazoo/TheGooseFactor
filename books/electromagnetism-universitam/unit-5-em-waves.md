# Unit V: Electromagnetic Waves

Light is what the electromagnetic field does when no charges are present. Setting the source to zero in $\nabla F = J$ gives the source-free equation $\nabla F = 0$, whose solutions are propagating waves. Geometric Algebra makes polarization, propagation direction, and the energy–momentum content of the field unusually transparent.
