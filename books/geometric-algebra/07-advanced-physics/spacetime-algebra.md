# Spacetime Algebra (STA)

Spacetime Algebra, developed by David Hestenes, is the application of Geometric Algebra to the 4D spacetime of relativity.

Instead of keeping space and time separate, or using complex matrix formulations, STA represents spacetime using a unified algebra of multivectors.

In STA, the basis vectors represent orthogonal directions in spacetime. This elegantly integrates the geometric structure of relativity, offering deep insights into Lorentz transformations, spin, and the Dirac equation.
