# Unit IV: Sequences & Series

Sequences and series are limits of partial sums, and they give us our first genuinely *multivector-valued* expansions. The headline result of this unit — the Taylor series — lets us expand the exponential of a bivector and recover the **rotor** $e^{B\theta/2}$, the GA object that performs rotations. Convergence is therefore not an abstract nicety here; it is what makes rotations, oscillations, and series solutions well defined.
