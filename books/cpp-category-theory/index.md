# C++: Category Theory & Verification

> A guide to applying Category Theory, functional paradigms, and formal verification concepts to C++, heavily inspired by the work and teachings of Bartosz Milewski.

## Why Category Theory in C++?

Category theory gives us a mathematical foundation for composability. In C++, template metaprogramming, concepts, and type traits can often feel like ad-hoc solutions to missing language features. Category theory provides the theoretical underpinning to understand what we are actually doing when we build abstractions like Monads, Functors, and Algebraic Data Types in modern C++.
