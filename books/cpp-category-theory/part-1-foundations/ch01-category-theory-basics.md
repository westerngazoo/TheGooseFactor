# Chapter 1: Category Theory Basics

Welcome to C++ Category Theory and Verification!

Category theory is often described as "the mathematics of mathematics". It focuses on the relationships and mappings between objects, rather than the internal structure of the objects themselves.

In programming, a category can be thought of as types (objects) and functions (morphisms) between them.
